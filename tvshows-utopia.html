Utopia (2013)

Live-action television drama series have become increasingly popular within the last two decades. Shows like Breaking Bad, Game of Thrones, and Sherlock have amassed massive fanbases over the years. Unfortunately, not all shows were that lucky. One show that I believe easily surpasses all of the aforementioned ones is the British thriller drama - Utopia.

The show centers around a woman named Jessica Hyde, who has discovered a secret conspiracy and is now being hunted down by hired assassins.

While being in some ways dated, Utopia is a brilliantly written work. The two existing seasons provide unprecedented depth to all of its primary characters, while also having a palpable sense of danger.

Unfortunately, the series got cancelled before it could properly conclude, leaving a rather open ending. Recently, there was an American remake, but it is nowhere near as good and I do not recommend that. However, the original UK series is strongly recommended by me to all fans of conspiracy theories and thrillers.
